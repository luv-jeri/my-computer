1. **aim for wow-level visual quality**
   - The product must look **beautiful, premium, and professional**, with a clear **wow factor**.
   - The design should feel **modern** with a strong **artistic touch**.

2. **prioritize speed and user experience first**
   - **Performance and UX are the top priorities** in every decision.
   - Interfaces should feel **fast, smooth, and responsive**.

3. **deliver an exceptional user interface**
   - Every screen should feel **cohesive, intentional, and easy to use**.
   - Layouts must be **clean, intuitive, and visually balanced**.

4. **use proper typography, fonts, and colors**
   - Always choose **legible, well-paired fonts** and maintain a clear **typographic hierarchy**.
   - Use a **consistent color system** with accessible contrast.

5. **add tasteful animations and microinteractions**
   - Use **subtle, meaningful animations** to enhance understanding and delight.
   - Add **microinteractions** (hover states, focus states, button feedback, transitions).

6. **design for full responsiveness**
   - The UI must work beautifully from **small mobile phones** to **ultra-wide monitors**.
   - No layout should feel broken, cramped, or awkward at any size.

7. **obsess over details**
   - Pay **close attention to every small part**: spacing, alignment, radii, shadows, icon size, states.
   - No rough edges: every component should feel **refined and deliberate**.

8. **balance artistry with usability**
   - The app should look like it was built by an **artist**, but **never at the expense of usability**.

9. **consistency across the entire experience**
   - Maintain consistent **patterns, components, and visual language** across all pages.
   - Reuse well-designed components rather than reinventing slightly different versions.

10. **always think: does this feel magical to the user?**
    - For every interaction, ask: Does this feel **delightful, smooth, and premium**?
